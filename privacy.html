Privacy

The short version: we don't keep your contracts, we don't sell anything, and no human reads what you upload.

What you upload

When you drop a TREC contract on the home page, the PDF is sent over HTTPS to our server, processed by AI, and used only to produce the review you see on screen. We do not share the file with anyone and we don't use it to train any model.

How long we keep it

Your uploaded PDF and the generated report are deleted within 24 hours. If you pay to unlock the full review, the recovery URL stays valid for the same 24-hour window — bookmark it if you want to come back.

What we collect about you

  • Standard server logs (IP, user agent, timestamp). Used for debugging and abuse prevention. Rotated regularly.
  • If you pay: Polar handles the card details directly as merchant of record. We never see or store your card number. Polar holds the transaction record per their own retention rules.
  • If you pay with Lightning: nothing identifying — just an invoice payment hash.

What we don't do

  • No accounts, no login, no password to lose.
  • No marketing emails. We don't have your address.
  • No analytics that track you across sites.
  • No selling or sharing of your contract content. Ever.

Cookies

The site uses a single short-lived session cookie if you start a checkout. No third-party tracking cookies.

Questions or takedowns

If you uploaded something by mistake or want it deleted before the 24-hour window expires, reach us at /contact.
